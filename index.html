ORDER GROCERIES

Fram is a circular service that ensures your kitchen is
always stocked with the essentials you need. Browse our
seasonal selection, choose your
items, and place your order online.

See our stock here

STOCK UP

Once your order arrives, unpack the produce and store it
however it works best for you. Hold onto the containers—
they’re yours to keep until your next order.

SCAN & REORDER

Ready for a refill? Grab the containers from your last order
and scan the QR code with our app or your phone’s
camera. This instantly adds the items back to your cart,
making reordering a breeze.

SWAP & REUSE

When we deliver your new order, simply leave your empty
containers outside. We’ll pick them up, clean them
thoroughly, and reuse them for future deliveries.
